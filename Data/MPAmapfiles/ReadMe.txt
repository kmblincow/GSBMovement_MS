This archive includes:

1. CaliforniaMPAs_180905.kmz - a Google Earth file displaying California state and federal marine protected areas (MPAs) and the three nautical mile maritime limit for California

2. MPA_CA_StateAndFederal_180905 - an ESRI shapefile including all state and federal MPAs represented individually

3. MPA_CA_StateAndFederal_180905_Merged - an ESRI shapefile including all state and federal MPAs with complimentary state and federal MPAs (exclusively around the Northern Channel Islands) merged together.

These MPAs are current as of January 1, 2019 - per California Fish and Game Commission action on August 22, 2018
http://nrm.dfg.ca.gov/FileHandler.ashx?DocumentID=160823&inline

Regulations for current California State MPAs are in California Code of Regulations, Title 14, Section 632
https://govt.westlaw.com/calregs/Document/I08D441E18E124A74B99747C081AD3495?viewType=FullText&originationContext=documenttoc&transitionType=CategoryPageItem&contextData=(sc.Default)

Regulations for complimentary federal MPAs around the Channel Islands are found in section 304(b) of the National Marine Sanctuaries Act (NMSA) 16 U.S.C. 1434(b)
https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2007/05/24/E7-10096/establishment-of-marine-reserves-and-a-marine-conservation-area-within-the-channel-islands-national
